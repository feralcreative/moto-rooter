Instructions

For Turn-by-Turn Navigation:

  • Click the View larger map button on each route to open in your native Google Maps app.
  • Once the app opens, if you you see a Preview » button in the bottom right instead of a Start ▲ button, it's because your current location is not the starting point of the route. Tap the ••• in the top right to Edit Stops, add Your Location, then grab it by the ☰ and slide it to the top of the list to make it the starting point.
